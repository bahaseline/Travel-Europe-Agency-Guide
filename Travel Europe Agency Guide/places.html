Amalfi Coast(Italy)

The alpine town of Ravello may not be for the faint of heart, but its ancient villas and stunning ocean views will be etched in your memory for years to come. Few cities rival Athens when it comes to historical importance. Santorini

A massive volcanic eruption around 1650 B.C. forced the center of what was then a single island to implode and succumb to the sea. Then you'll have plenty of time to learn there's more to these comely dots of the Cyclades than meets the eye.
